America the Beautiful

This site has been developed to provide educational content on US National Parks. Our team hopes that the interactive content will encourage trips to parks and ultimately drive awareness on the importance of conserving our land and protecting key habitats to preserve our native plants and animals.

Included you will find:

A timeline that visualizes when national parks were established

A map that visualizes national park visitor counts
